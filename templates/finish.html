Why does this matter?

In the age of AI, it's becoming more and more difficult to spot the difference between reality and something generated by AI. While this challenge may seem somewhat fun, AI can be dangerous in some circumstances, especially in the context of spreading misinformation and "fake" news.


This site is meant to help spread awareness and let people know that they should be weary of where they get their news and the credibility of what they see or hear.
